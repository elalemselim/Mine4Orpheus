# Hackclub Campfire Ottawa - Mine4Orpheus
Fun little game for the 2026 Hack Club Campfire event in Ottawa.  Theme - Beneath The Surface

<img width="612" height="408" alt="Orpeus Shack" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d7c86265-bbe5-409a-b403-829e577cb539" />


You’re a mole that digs for ores to make a living. Your boss, Orpheus, pays you based on which ore you bring him (Diamond, Gold, Silver, Bronze). You start above the surface, where you can choose to go below the surface and dig through the ground to start searching for ores. Once you finish digging and wish to go sell your findings, you choose the option to teleport back up again and find Orpheus, where you will offer your different ores in exchange for money.
